  2. decay vi 腐烂 corrupt v 腐败 delay v 耽搁，延迟

  3. deceive vt 欺骗 deceive(cheat) sb into doing

  4. decline v&n 下降；vt 婉拒 -cline 弯曲 decline an offer decline an invitation sb incline to do 倾向做 recline 依靠 + on

  5. decrease v&n increase v&n

  6. dedicate -devote commit vt 1) do/make；2）承诺 commit a crime/commit suicide be commited/dedicated/devoted to +N 致力于

  7. deem v think 认为

  8. deficit n -deficient adj 不足的 n 赤字 efficient adj 有效率的

  9. define vt 下定义 -definition n 定义 confine to 限制 refine vt 精炼 refined sugar 精糖 refine your language

  11. deliberate adj 故意的；从容的

  12. delighted adj 开心的，高兴的 -delight vt

  13. deliver vt 投放，送 deliver milk/newspaper/a speech delivery n the pxpress service

  14. democratic adj 民主的 -democracy n

  20. derive derive from 源自 - come from源自 - stem from - originate from

  21. descendant descend - ascend vi 上升 descendant 后代，下降

  22. deserve vt 值得，该受 conserve vt 保守，保护 -conservative adj 保守的 -conservation n 保护 observe vt 观察；遵守，vi 说 reserve vt 预定，保留；n 储备，储存，保留 reservation 预定 reserved 有所保留的 oill reserved/reserve fund preserve vt 保存；腌制 preserved meat a well-preserved lady

  23. designate vt 指定，标明，命名

  24. desirable adj 渴望的，想要的，理解的 -desive vt 渴望 to do desirable jobs理想的工作

  25. desperate adj 绝望的，孤注一掷的 desperate vt 绝望 - despaired

  26. despise vt 蔑视，轻视

  27. detect vt 发现-detective n 侦探

  28. determine vt 决心 I am determined to do 决心做

  29. devise vt 设计 -device n 设备

  30. dictate vt 听写；口授；命令 dictation n 命令 -dic 说 dictation dictionary n 字典 contradict vt 矛盾

  31. dilemma n 进退两难

  32. diligent adj 勤奋的 I am a hard-working/diligent/industrious student industry n 工业；勤奋 industrial adj 工业的 industrous adj

  33. diminish vt 减少；减小 -mini 小 miniskirt

  34. disastrous adj -disaster n 灾难 This is a disaster This can cause disastrous consequence

  35. discern vt 辨认，确认 - identify vt 约束，管束

  36. dsicipline n 学科 This a cross discipline 纪律 classroom discipline/military discipline self-discipline 自律性

  37. disclose

- enclose vt 圈起来 - enclosure n close vt 关闭 adj disclose vt 透露，揭露，泄露

  1. discriminate n 歧视 racial/age/sex/gender discriminate

  2. disgrace vt 不雅，是丢脸 Grace n 优雅 graceful/graceless/disgraceful

  3. disguise v&n 伪装 a blessing in disguise put yourself in other‘s shoes 换位思考

  4. dismiss vt 打发，解散；不理会 lay off 解雇 you are dismissed

  5. display demonstrate vt&n be on display window display 橱窗展示

  6. dispose vi +of 处置，处理（旧物）-disposal n at sb’s disposal the car is now at my dispose

  7. disrupt -rupt=break bankrupt/corrupt/erupt/interrupt 打断，干扰 disrupt vt 破坏 disruption 打扰

  8. distinct adj 不同的+from;清楚的 The smell of beer is distinct from wine

  9. distinguish vt 1)区分 distinguish A from B；distinguish between…and please distinguish the good from the bad 2)使卓越 He has distinguish himself many time in war distinguished adj 卓越的 a distinguished guest 嘉宾

  10. distract 分散 vt from 注意力等 attract- contract- extract- abstract- tract：draw

  11. distribute vt 分发，分派，分布- distribution contribute attribute to 归因于

  12. diverse adj 各种各样的 - diversity n 多样性 biodiversity n 生物多样性

  13. dividend n 分红 allowance bonus divide vt 分割

  14. domestic adj 国内的，家的 domestic departure 国内离岗 domestic violence 家暴

  15. dominant vt 占主导，占主控 -dominant adj

  16. dramatic adj 戏剧化的 - drama n 戏剧 considerable adj 可观的 大量的

  17. due adj 应到的 is due to ;到期的；因为；适当的 after due 适当的 consideration，he decided to undertake MBA program

  18. dynamic adj 动态的；有活力的 energetic/vigorous adj 有活力的 dynamics n 动力学；动态

  2. echo n 回声 vt 回响 narcissus 水仙花

  6. elaborate adj 精心准备的；复杂的 an elaborate dinner complex complicated sophisticated intricate elaborate

  12. embody vt 体现

  13. emerge vi 出现 + from -arise an emerging industry 新兴行业 emergency emergence emergent n 紧急情况 n 出现 adj 紧急的 an emergency airport/brake/room/fund/exit

  14. emigrate vi 移民 migrate vi 迁徙 - immigrate 移民 migrant n -immigrant - emigrant n 移民

  15. eminent prominent exceptional extraordinary distinguished outstanding 杰出的，卓越的 excellent execl vi 优秀+at 好，擅长，优秀

  17. encounter vt 遇见，偶遇；遭遇 -meet

  18. endeavour vi&n 努力 endeavor to do 努力去做

  19. endow vt 赋予才能，财富 be endow with

  21. engage vt 忙于；从事；吸引 be engaged in 忙于；从事 the toy can engage the attention of the kid 订婚 engagement ring 订婚戒指

  27. enterprise n 企业；进取心 state-owned enterprise 国有企业 corporation n 公司 He is a young man of enterprise

  28. enthusiastic adj 热情的 enthusiasm

  29. epidemic n 流行病 covid-19；coronavirus pandemic

  30. equivalent n 等同物，等价物 -of/to -que:相等；平等 adj -equality n

  31. erase vt 擦除；去除 eraser n 橡皮

  32. erect correct vt 改正，纠正（错误）；矫正 -rect 直立 rectify vt 纠正；矫正

  33. erupt vt 爆发，喷发（火山等）eruption n

  34. essential adj 基本重要的 essence n 本质 in essence 从本质上讲 重要的 critical- crucial- vital

  35. estate real estate房地产 property n 财产；地产

  36. esteem self-esteem 自尊 self-confidence self-discipline

  37. estimate vt 估计 estimation over/underestimate

  38. eternal adj 永恒的

  40. evacuate vt 撤离，疏散

  41. evade vt 逃跑 - invade vt 入侵，侵略

  42. evaluate vt 估价，评估 vale(价值等) assess vt 评估（质量等）

  43. eventually 最终 finally/in the end/at last

  44. evident adj 明显的，显而易见的 evidence n 证据 -proof 明显地：evidently；apparently；obviously；self-evident 不言而喻的

  45. evolve vi 进化发展 - evolution n 进化 shenzhen has evolved from a small fishing village into a modern big city -volve 转 involve vt 卷入牵连；包含- involution 内卷 I don’t want to be involved in the case revolve v 旋转 - revolution n 革命

- exclusive adj 排他的，独有的，专有的 an exclusive interview 独家专访 The rent is &350，breakfast included/excluded=inclusive/exclusive of breakfast

  1. executive n 行政官员；执行 CEO- chief executive officer sports executive

  2. exert vt 施加（压力、影响） exert pressure/influence on

  3. exempt adj免除的 be exempt from 免于+punishment duty-free

  4. expel repel- compel- dispel -pel 推 pull/push expel vt 驱逐 compel vt 逼迫迫使 -be compelled to do 不得不 repel v 排斥 water and fire repel each other dispel vi 消散（烦恼）

  6. expenditure expenditure- expense- expend expenditure n 花销花费（不可数） expense n 花销花费（复数）

  7. expose vt 暴露于；接触到+to be exposed to Don’t expose your skin to strong sunlight college students can be exposed to many foreign cultures

  8. extent to some extent 在一定程度上 health is important to us health，to some extent，is critical to us

  9. external adj 外部的 - internal adj 内部的

  10. extinguish vt 熄灭灭火 - distinguish 区分

  11. extract adj 灭绝的 many animals have been extinct

  12. extreme n 极端 don’t go the extreme extremely adv very 非常地
