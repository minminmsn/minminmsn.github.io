英语词汇导学–顾巍老师

C开头重点单词盘点

  1. calculate vt 计算，估计；vi 认为；reckon估计

  2. campaign n 战役；活动 camp n 营地 sales promotion campaign election campaign

  3. cancel vt 取消 -flight abandon vt abolish bubbles

  4. candidate n 候选人，考生

  5. capable adj 有能力的 be capable of -capacity n 容积容量 capable adj 有能力的 - able adj be able to do be capable of doing able - ability capable - capability n 能力

  6. capacity n 能力，容量；volume 体积，卷 This auditorium演讲厅，报告厅has a seating capacity of 500 auditorium -ium 建筑物 stadium 体育馆，体育场（漏天的）；gymnasium健身房 oceanarium 海洋馆；aquarium 水族馆

  7. captive n 俘虏 abuse captive 虐俘

  8. capture vt 占据；抓 capture a city 占据一个城市 capture a thief 抓了一个贼 capture sb’s heart赢得sb的心 Recapture 夺回，赢回，再体验 Government troops soon recaptured the island. He was trying to recapture the happiness of his youth.

  9. career n 事业 care 小心，认真

  10. cargo n （运输途中的）货物；a cargo plane/ship；freight（美语）（运输途中的）货物

  11. case n 情况；案例；病例；例子 1)in case of +名词/in case +句子：以防；万一 Dial 119 in case of fire. Take an umbrella with you in case it rains. 2)in…case在情况下 in any case/in no case I will give up in no case meet/satisfy/cater to （满足）one’s needs

  12. casual adj 偶然的，偶尔，邂逅 a casual glance/meeting

  13. casualty n 伤亡 casualty list 伤亡名单

  14. cater vi 迎合 cater to/for；cater to low tastes迎合低级趣味 cater to universal tastes 迎合大众口味

  15. caution vi 发出警告- cautious adj 谨慎的 - careful

  16. cease vt 停止；cease fire 停火

  17. celebrate v 庆祝 祝贺 赞美

  18. ceremony n 典礼，仪式，礼节

  19. certificate n 证明材料；a birth certificate 出生证明；health certificate 健康证明

  20. certify v 证实；certain adj 肯定的 -cert：确定的 肯定的

  21. challenge v & n 挑战

  22. characteristic n 特征 adj 特有的；character 人物，性格；leading characters 领衔主演

  23. charge vt & n 收费；指责；负责 charge sb 50 pence；指控 charge sb with sth = accuse sb of sth n 收费 additional charges

  24. chase vt 追逐

  25. check v 检查核对 n（美式英语）支票 by check；in cash；vt 克制，抑制；check one’s impulse 克制冲动

  26. cheque n 支票

  27. cherish vt 珍惜；怀有（希望）-value cherish friendship/life/love… cherish the hope

  28. chief adj 首席的 CEO=Chief Executive Officer

  29. circular adj 圆形的，环形的；circle n 圆圈；cycle n.v. 循环 recycle再循环

  30. circulate v. 流通 blood circulation 血液循环

  31. circumstance n 环境，情形（多用复数）；under/in these circumstances；under no circumstances 绝不 I will give up in no circumstances.

  32. civil adj 1）文明的 2）国内的 3）民的 civilization n 文明 civil tongue 文明的语言 mother tongue Civil War 内战 a civil case 民事案件 a criminal case 刑事案件 civilian n 平民

  33. claim vt & n 宣称；索要 acclaim v 欢呼 He is an acclaimed writer. He claimed to be the CEO of the company. He claimed that he would attend the meeting. Every child has the right to claim education. claim compensation 赔偿 for my loss. The hurricane 飓风 has claimed six innocent无辜的people. claim-acclaim-proclaim reclaim 回收，改造

  34. clarify vt 澄清 clear clarify your opinions

  35. clash n 冲突，和…冲突；打斗，分歧；争论 a clash of interests/opinions/cultures利益冲突；意见分歧；文化差异 be in conflict/collision/clash with…和…冲突 be in accord/agreement/line with…和..一致 a head-on clash between the two leaders over education policy

  36. classical adj 古典；classis 经典；classify分级，分类；first-class teacher

  37. climax n 高潮 highlight

  38. cling vi 粘紧，附着，坚持；cling to /adhere to /stick to 坚持 cling to a principle 原则

  39. clue n 线索 -cue n 提示 暗示

  40. clumsy adj 笨拙的

  41. coarse adj 粗糙的，粗俗的，沙哑的；a coarse throat 沙哑的喉咙；coarse foods粗粮 coarse/refined wine劣酒/佳酿；coarse manners粗俗行为

  42. code n 编码，代码 zip code 邮编（encode/decode）

  43. coincidence adj 巧合的 （coincidence n巧合）-incident n 事件 -accident n 事故 by accident=by chance 偶然的 He is to blame for the accident.

  44. collaborate v 协作合作 -cooperate v 合作

  45. collapse vt 崩溃，倒塌 He collapsed at the bad news. lapse n 小错 a lapse of tongue/pen 口误/笔误 blunder n 大错

  46. colleageue n 同事；league 同盟；workmate同事；deskmate 同桌 classmate；schoolmate

  47. collide vi 相互碰撞，冲突；clash；air crash

  48. combat v 打斗，与…战斗 campaign 战役 fight

  49. combine vt 联合 combine A with B

  50. comfort vt & n （使）舒服 comfortable 舒服的

  51. command

  52. comment vi & n 评论 + on make comments on comment on No comments.无可奉告

  53. commercial adj 商业的 commerce n 商业 E-commerce 电子商务 commodity n 商品

  54. commit vt 1）do/make 做 commit a crime犯罪 commit suicide自杀 n 2）承诺 commit oneself to 致力于 He committed himself to helping the poor all his life. committed adj 尽职尽责的 commitment n 承诺，责任 -mit admit/commit/emit（释放，排放）/limit/omit省略/submit提交/transmit vt 传播

  55. commodity n 商品

  56. communicate communicate vi +with communication n 交流

  57. community community n 社区

  58. companion n 伙伴 company accompany vt 陪伴

  59. compare vt 比较 compare…with comparable adj 可比较的；类似的 The comparable type of cars will cost more at home.

  60. compassion n 同期 -passion n激情；热情+for He has passion for music.

  61. compatible adj 兼容的 compatible with; pat 轻轻拍打

  62. compensate vi +for 赔偿 claim compensation for my loss

  63. competent adj 有能力的 -fierce激烈的 compete vi 竞争 competition n 竞争 competitive adj 有竞争力的 competitiveness n 竞争力

  64. competition n 竞争；compete with 竞争；compete against sb 和sb竞争 competitive adj 有竞争力对；competitor n 竞争对手

  65. compile vt 1)编篡 2）罗列，编排；pile v. 堆； compile a list of dishes/books 罗列一个菜/书单

  66. complain vi 抱怨，投诉 complaint n 抱怨投诉 make a complaint 投诉 complain of/about sth 抱怨 He made a complaint of the smartphone. plain adj 明白的 n 平原

  67. complement n 补充 vt 补充 complete 完成

  68. complex complicate-sophisticated-intricate elaborate adj 精心准备的 The elaborate dinner on the New Year’s Eve.

  69. complicated adj 复杂的（形式）

  71. comply comply with 和…保持一致，符合，服从

  72. compose compose-consist-constitute-comprise v 组成 compose vt 组成，构成；创作 be composed of Our class is composed of 50 students. composer n 作曲家 composition n 作文 consist vi +of Our class consists of 50 student. constitute vt 构成，组成 A week comprises 7 days. 7 days comprise a week. be made up of 由组成 Our class is made up of 50 students. compose/consist of /constitute/make up of

  77. concede -ceed/-cess 走 proceed/process access -cede/-cession recede/recession concede vi 让步
